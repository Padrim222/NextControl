🔑 Key Improvements in this Wireframe

  • ✅ Mobile bottom nav — Sales teams can navigate without a hamburger menu
  • ✅ Clickable timeline items — Navigate to view submission details with chevron affordance
  • ✅ Sparkline in Score stat — Shows trend at a glance without navigating away
  • ✅ Formatted dates — "19 fev" instead of "2024-02-19"
  • ✅ Score badges on timeline — Quick scan of performance history
  • ✅ Desktop: 2-col layout — CTA alongside feedback for efficient use of space
  • ✅ Quick Actions strip — Easy access to Evolução and Coach on mobile
  • ✅ Relative timestamps — "há 2h" for feedback freshness
